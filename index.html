The Algorithmic Dungeon

An interactive visualization of Dynamic Programming and Greedy Algorithms

The Dungeon

DP: Min Path Sum

Find the path with the lowest stamina cost from Top-Left to Bottom-Right.

The Loot Pouch

Greedy: Fractional Knapsack

Take the best items by Value/Weight ratio. You can take fractions of items (e.g., Gold Dust).

The Armory

DP: 0/1 Knapsack

Take whole items only to maximize value within capacity. Hover over the final answer to backtrack.
